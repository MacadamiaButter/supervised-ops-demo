# Rent payment & billing
Rent is due on the 1st, with a 5-day grace period; a $75 late fee applies
starting the 6th. Accepted methods: ACH/bank transfer and card (2.9%
surcharge) through the tenant portal, or a mailed check to the Larkspur
Ave office. Partial payments are applied to oldest balances first per the
lease. NSF/returned payments incur a $35 fee. Tenants disputing a charge
should use the portal's "billing dispute" form so it's tracked -- don't
promise a credit or waiver in a reply; that requires manager sign-off.
