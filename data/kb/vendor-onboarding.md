# Vendor onboarding & work orders
New maintenance vendors must provide a W-9, proof of liability insurance
($1M general liability minimum), and a signed W-9/vendor agreement before
being added to the dispatch rotation. Work orders are assigned through
the vendor portal with property access instructions and tenant contact
info; vendors are expected to text an ETA window before arrival and
close out the work order with photos. Payment terms are net-15 on
approved invoices submitted through the portal, not by email attachment.
