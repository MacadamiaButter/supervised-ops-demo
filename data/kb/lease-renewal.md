# Lease renewal process
Renewal offers go out 90 days before lease end via the tenant portal and
email, with 60 days to accept the offered rate, request a shorter renewal
term (6 or 9 months, +$50/mo admin), or give notice to vacate (30 days
minimum, 60 preferred). Rent increases are capped at the greater of 5% or
the local ordinance cap, whichever is lower, and are explained in the
renewal packet with a comparables summary. Tenants in good standing
(on-time pay history, no open violations) are eligible for a $0
administrative renewal fee; a past-due balance or an open compliance item
must be resolved before a renewal offer is issued.
