# Move-out & security deposit process
30 days' written notice required (60 preferred). A pre-move-out
walkthrough can be scheduled in the final two weeks to flag anything
fixable before the final inspection. Security deposit disposition
(itemized, with receipts for any deductions) is mailed within the state's
required window after keys are returned -- currently 21 days in our
jurisdiction. Normal wear and tear is not deductible; unreturned keys,
excess cleaning, and damage beyond wear and tear are. Full move-out
checklist is on the tenant portal under "Resources".
