# Maintenance response SLAs
Emergency (no heat below 55F in winter, active water leak, no electricity,
lockout with safety concern, gas smell): dispatched within 2 hours,
24/7. Urgent (no hot water, major appliance failure, single AC unit down
in summer): next business day. Routine (leaky faucet, squeaky door,
cosmetic repairs, filter changes): scheduled within 3-5 business days.
Tenants are notified by text and portal message when a vendor is
assigned, with an ETA window. Gas smell or suspected gas leak: tenant
should evacuate and call the gas utility emergency line directly before
contacting us.
