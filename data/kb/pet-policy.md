# Pet policy
Cats and dogs are allowed at all Bluejay-managed properties except the
Larkspur Row townhomes (no-pet buildings per HOA covenant). Pet deposit
is $300 (partially refundable, condition-dependent) plus $35/month pet
rent per animal, max 2 pets per unit. Breed restrictions follow our
insurer's list (available on request) -- generally large guard-breed
dogs need a temperament reference. Emotional support and service animals
are exempt from pet rent and deposit with proper documentation on file;
route these to the leasing manager, don't quote pet fees.
