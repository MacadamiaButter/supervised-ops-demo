# Parking & storage
Each unit is assigned one covered parking space; a limited number of
uncovered second spaces are available for $45/month, waitlist by
building. Guest parking is 72-hour max and must display a dashboard
guest tag from the office or portal. Unregistered or expired-tag
vehicles are subject to towing at the owner's expense per posted signage.
Storage units (where available) are a separate rider, $60-95/month
depending on size, and are not included in the base lease.
