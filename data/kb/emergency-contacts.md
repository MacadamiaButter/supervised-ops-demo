# Emergency contacts
Life-safety emergency (fire, medical, gas leak, break-in in progress):
call 911 first, then notify the office. After-hours property
emergencies (flooding, no heat in freezing weather, lockouts with a
safety concern): call the 24/7 emergency line (503) 555-0199, which pages
the on-call maintenance vendor. Do not attempt electrical or gas repairs
yourself. For non-life-safety issues that can wait until morning, use the
tenant portal so the request is timestamped and routed normally.
